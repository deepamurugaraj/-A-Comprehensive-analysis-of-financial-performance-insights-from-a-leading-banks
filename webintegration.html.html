A comprehensive analysis of financial performance: Insights from a leading banks.

This session introduces bank financial statement and provides a traditional,ratio-base procedure for analyzing bank financial performance using historical data. It demonstrates the interrelationship between the income statement and balance sheet and describes the risk and return trade-off underlying management decisions. Data are provided that compare the performance characteristics of small banks verus large banks and differentiate between high and low performance. Financial performance analysis is multifaceted approach to evaluating a company’s financial performance. It is the process of examining a business enterprise’s financial records,statement ,tools,and process. Here are some of the things financial performance analysis looks at: ? Working capital: It is the difference between the companies’ current assets and liability. ? Financial structure: It is the mix of equality and other portfolios offered by the company. ? Analyzing activities: It analyses business functions like costing, pricing,accounts,etc.

Conclusion

Financial performance analysis determines a company’s health and stability, providing an understanding of how the company conducts its business. But it is important to know that financial performance analysis has its limitation as well. Different according methods adopted by different firms changes the visible health and profit levels for either better or worse. Different analysts may get different results from the same the information. Hence, we must conclude that financial performance analysis in banks is only one of the tools while taking an investment decisions.
